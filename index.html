NOTE

Below are realistic starting weight estimations assuming:

  • Beginner to early-intermediate lifter
  • Decent form on main movements
  • Not currently lifting very heavy

These are starting ranges. If you can exceed them cleanly, do so. If they feel too heavy, scale down 10–15%.

Monday
Push
Chest + Triceps
  • Barbell Bench Press 30–40 kg total (incl. bar) 4 × 6–8
  • Incline Dumbbell Press 12–16 kg per dumbbell 3 × 8–10
  • Machine Chest Press 25–40 kg 3 × 10–12
  • Dips Bodyweight (add weight once at 12) 3 × 8–12
  • Rope Pushdowns 15–25 kg 3 × 12–15
  • Overhead Tricep Ext. (Cable) 15–20 kg 3 × 10–12
Tuesday
Pull
Back + Biceps
  • Pull-ups Bodyweight (assist if <6 reps) 4 × 6–10
  • Barbell Row 30–40 kg 3 × 8–10
  • Seated Cable Row 30–45 kg 3 × 10–12
  • Face Pulls 15–25 kg 3 × 12–15
  • Barbell Curl 20–25 kg 3 × 8–10
  • Incline Dumbbell Curl 8–12 kg per hand 3 × 10–12
  • Hammer Curl 10–14 kg per hand 3 × 10–12
Wednesday
Legs
  • Back Squat 35–50 kg 4 × 5–8
  • Romanian Deadlift 40–60 kg 3 × 8–10
  • Leg Press 80–120 kg (machine dependent) 3 × 10–12
  • Leg Curl 25–40 kg 3 × 10–12
  • Calf Raises 40–80 kg 4 × 12–20
Friday
Arms + Shoulders
(Priority)
  • Close-Grip Bench 30–35 kg 4 × 6–8
  • EZ Bar Curl 20–25 kg 4 × 8–10
  • Skull Crushers 15–25 kg 3 × 10–12
  • Cable Curl 15–25 kg 3 × 12–15
  • Lateral Raises 6–10 kg per dumbbell 4 × 15–20
  • Rear Delt Fly (Machine) 20–35 kg 3 × 15
Saturday
Upper Power
  • Overhead Press (Barbell) 20–30 kg 4 × 5–6
  • Weighted Pull-ups Bodyweight (add 2.5 kg once strong) 4 × 5–6
  • Dumbbell Bench 16–20 kg per dumbbell 3 × 8
  • T-Bar Row 30–50 kg 3 × 8
  • Barbell Curl + Dips Superset Same as earlier ranges 3 × 8–12

Key Principles

The Real Growth Lever

Critical at 60 kg. At your height, lifting alone won’t build big arms unless you:

🥩

Eat in a Surplus

  • ~2,800–3,000 kcal/day to start
  • Protein: ~120g daily

If scale isn’t moving up ~0.25–0.5 kg/week, increase food.

📈

Track Progression

If your bench moves from 35 kg to 60 kg over a few months, your arms will grow. Strength is the engine for hypertrophy at this stage.

💤

Sleep Properly

Aim for 7–9 hours a night. Growth hormone matters substantially more when you’re this lean.
